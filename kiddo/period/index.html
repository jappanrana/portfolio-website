🌷 My Period Comfort Day 🌷

A calm plan to help you feel better

🌞 Morning

  • 🛁 Take a warm shower or bath
  • 🍌 Light breakfast: banana, nuts, herbal tea
  • 🚶 Gentle walk for 10–15 mins
  • 🧘‍♀️ Light stretching or yoga

🌤️ Midday

  • 🍵 Sip ginger or chamomile tea
  • 🥗 Eat magnesium-rich lunch: leafy greens + seeds
  • 📖 Relax with a book or nap
  • 💧 Drink water (skip salty snacks)

🌙 Evening

  • 🛀 Warm bath to relax before sleep
  • 🍫 A small piece of dark chocolate (yes!)
  • 💤 Light stretching, then sleep early

🍴 Foods to Eat & Drink

  • 🍫 Dark chocolate (rich in magnesium)
  • 🥬 Leafy greens
  • 🥜 Nuts & seeds
  • 🍌 Bananas
  • 🍵 Ginger tea
  • 🌼 Chamomile tea
  • 💧 Water (stay hydrated)
  • 🚫 Reduce salty foods

🧘‍♀️ Gentle Things to Do

  • 🛁 Warm bath or shower
  • 🚶 Light walk in fresh air
  • 🧘 Stretching or gentle yoga
  • 📖 Read, relax, or nap
  • 🎧 Listen to soft music
  • 💤 Sleep early
